Solar Solutions for Homes in Pakistan (2025)

We provide reliable and affordable solar panel installation services across Pakistan. With rising electricity costs and continuous load-shedding, solar energy is becoming essential for every home. Our team guides you from system design to final installation.

Why Solar Energy Is Becoming Essential in Pakistan

Electricity prices are increasing every year, and load-shedding is common in many cities. Solar energy helps reduce monthly bills and gives long-term savings. Pakistan receives strong sunlight throughout the year — making solar energy one of the best investments for homeowners.

Solar System Cost Overview (3kW–10kW)

Solar system cost depends on panel quality, inverter type, and installation materials. Smaller homes usually need 3kW or 5kW, while bigger homes require 10kW. Prices vary depending on city and brand availability.

Benefits of Installing Solar for Home

  • Lower Electricity Bills: Reduce 60–90% of your monthly bill.
  • Backup Availability: With batteries, you can run home appliances during load-shedding.
  • Increase Property Value: Solar-installed homes have higher resale value.
